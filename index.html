Follow Back?

1. Go to your Twitter profile page in a browser.

2. Click the following tab and scroll down until no more people who are following you load.

3. Click an empty part of the page and press ctrl+A, ctrl+C to copy the contents.

4. Come back to this page, click the top box and press ctrl+V, which will paste it all in. Below it will tell you who you don’t follow back.

Note: You must be logged into your Twitter account when you copy the data.
